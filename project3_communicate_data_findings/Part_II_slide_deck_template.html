Part II - Influence of age on the duration, the relationship between bike share for all trip and member age category, the predominance of men and the predominance of subscibers, followed by the start periode of the day and the start day

by Jonas Levis SOH KAMTE¶

Investigation Overview¶

This presentation provide insights on the influence of age on the duration, the influence of gender on duration, the predominance of men and the predominance of subscibers, followed by the most dominant start periode of the day and the start day.

Dataset Overview¶

The Ford GoBike System Data is a set of informations about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.
